A non - veg boutique restaurant that blends ancient-style architecture with modern amenities for a memorable stay in the Sun City. Our upscale property provides exclusively vegetarian dining options and proximity to destinations like the Clock Tower and Mandore Garden. When you aren’t sifting through 15th-century artefacts at Fort, sit amidst the lush greens on our hotel grounds to admire the gold leafing, Haveli-style carvings and regal red-sandstone façade.

Before exploring area attractions, have a hearty and healthy breakfast in our All Day Dining restaurant, and afterward, enjoy an endcap at one of our upcoming bars. You can connect to free Wi-Fi to find the perfect spot for a family picnic or ask our 24-hour concierge to recommend a destination for you. If you’re in town for work, book our Banquet Hall to give a professional presentation, or stop by the bar to conduct a more casual business meeting. When the day is done, swim in the pool or unwind in the on-site SVA Spa. Other amenities include:

  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Two restaurants - Ekatra All Day Dining and Abhiras rooftop Indian restaurant (Abhiras opening soon)
  • Two bars - 1459 and J Bar (both opening soon)
  • 24-hour room service
  • Meeting facilities - Four banquet halls with a total area of 30,000 sq. ft.
  • Outdoor pool, open 07:00-20:00 (summer) and 07:00-19:00 (winter)
  • On-site SVA Spa, open 09:00-21:00, gymnasium open 06:00-22:00
  • 24-hour airport transfer
